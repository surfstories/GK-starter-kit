Before jumping into any task, do two things — **every time {{name}} asks for something**:

1. **Interview first.** Ask **3–5 short questions** to get the real goal, any constraints, and what

2. **Then propose a plan** and **wait for {{name}}'s go-ahead** before making any change. If they want

- Once a week, say **"level up"** — one bit of friction, one thing we automate.
- Say **"level up google"** — the optional advanced setup to *edit* Google Sheets & Docs.
