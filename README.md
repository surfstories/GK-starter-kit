# Starter Kit — START HERE / НАЧНИ ЗДЕСЬ

Turn Claude into **your own AI workspace** — a single folder that gets smarter about you the more
you use it. In a few minutes you'll **connect your Google** (Drive, and read your Docs & Sheets),
build your first little project, and turn it into a one-word command — all guided, step by step.

**You don't need to install anything except the Claude app. No coding. No API keys. No terminal.**
You can always say "no" — nothing on your computer changes without your OK, and nothing is shared
anywhere unless you approve a connection.

---

## 🇷🇺 Русский

**Как начать**

1. Открой эту папку в приложении **Claude** (десктоп-приложение → вкладка **Claude Code**). Нет
   приложения? Скачай на [claude.ai/download](https://claude.ai/download) и войди в свой аккаунт.
2. В поле сообщения напиши и нажми Enter:

   > сделай онбординг

3. Дальше ассистент всё сделает **вместе с тобой**, на твоём языке:
   - поздоровается и задаст пару коротких вопросов (можно отвечать **цифрой** из меню);
   - поможет **подключить Google** (Диск — а значит и чтение твоих Google Документов и Таблиц) в
     одну пару кликов, через безопасный вход в браузере (**без ключей, без терминала**);
   - соберёт твой **первый проект** и превратит его в команду одним словом;
   - наведёт порядок и подскажет, как работать дальше.

*Хочешь потом **редактировать** Google Таблицы и Документы (а не только читать)? Это отдельная,
более техническая настройка — позже просто скажи ассистенту: **«прокачай Google»**.*

## 🇬🇧 English

**How to start**

1. Open this folder in the **Claude app** (desktop app → **Claude Code** tab). No app yet? Get it at
   [claude.ai/download](https://claude.ai/download) and sign in.
2. In the message box, type and press Enter:

   > onboard me

3. The assistant does everything **with you**, in your language:
   - says hi and asks a couple of short questions (you can answer with a **number** from a menu);
   - helps you **connect Google** (Drive — which also lets it read your Google Docs & Sheets) in a
     couple of clicks, via a safe browser sign-in (**no keys, no terminal**);
   - builds your **first project** and turns it into a one-word command;
   - tidies up and shows you how to keep going.

*Want to later **edit** Google Sheets & Docs (not just read them)? That's a separate, more technical
setup — just tell the assistant **"level up google"** when you're ready.*

## More information (reference)

**Share this kit:** the friendly download page (RU/EN, with the Download button and steps) is at
https://claude.ai/code/artifact/eb9b7d69-bc7e-4746-ae36-7e6defb84c0d — that's the link to hand to
people. Techies can also use the repo directly: github.com/surfstories/GK-starter-kit

**What you need:** the **Claude desktop app** (it has the Connectors that make the Google step
one-click). If you only use Claude Code in a terminal, switch to the desktop app for this kit.

**What onboarding does:** it runs in your language (picked up from your first message), greets you,
asks a few short questions, helps you connect Google with a safe one-click sign-in, builds your first
project and first skill with you, then hands you a clean, personal workspace and a short guide for
what to do next.

**What's in the kit:**
- `CLAUDE.md` / `AGENTS.md` — the rules your assistant follows (Claude reads the first, Codex the second).
- `system/` — `onboarding-procedure.md` (the guided flow), `connections.md`, `decisions.md`,
  `level-up-method.md`, `profile.example.md`, `guides/` (the optional "level up google" guide),
  `templates/`.
- `.claude/skills/` — `onboard` ("onboard me"), `create-skill`, `deepen-profile`, `level-up`.
- `Projects/` — where every project you build lives, one folder each.

**Good to know:**
- **One task = one new chat.** Start each new thing in a fresh chat; files it makes go in their own
  folder under `Projects/`.
- **It compounds.** The more you work from this folder, the more your assistant knows about you and
  the faster it helps.
- **Stuck?** Just ask the assistant — paste a screenshot and say "what next?". Still stuck? Reply to
  the post / message the person who shared this kit with you.
